Ace of Angels (AOA)

The best Kpop girl group of FNC

AOA (에이오에이)

  • Why the name of AOA?
  • It is the acronym of Ace of Angels
  • Members: Seven
  • Debut: August 08, 2012
  • Fanclub Name:Elvis
  • Activity Period: 2012-2019

Members

  • Choa (Main Vocalist, Dancer)
  • Jimin (Leader, Main Reaper, Dancer)
  • Yuna (Main Vocalist, Dancer)
  • Hyejeong (Vocalist, Visual, Dancer)
  • Mina (Vocalist, Reaper, Dancer)
  • Seolhyun (Visual, Vocalist, Main Dancer)
  • Chanmi(Maknae, Reaper, Main Dancer)

Here are some curiosities of AOA

  • They were the first girl group of FNC
  • In 2013 the group did not receive salary due to the low revenue of sales that did not covered the agency investment
  • The main song of their comeback 'Miniskirt' entered to the Top 10 of the online charts and Hanteo chart, being the most succesfull single of the group, they even won their first trophy in Inkigayo
  • In 2016 their japanese comeback 'Give me the Love' achieved the #1 in the Daily Chart of Shibuya, plus the #1 in Oricon charts
  • In 2017 they cameback with their first album 'Angel's Knock' winning two throphies in Showchampion
  • In 2019 the group participated in the contest 'Queendom' receiving positive feedback
  • After a several series of bullying controversies the group disbanded.
